Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Information We Collect

CalmSphere collects the following information:

  • Authentication Data: OAuth tokens for YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok (stored locally in your browser session)
  • Video Metadata: Information about videos you process (titles, descriptions, tags) - stored locally
  • Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to improve the service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate with third-party platforms (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok)
  • Process and upload your videos as requested
  • Improve the Service based on usage patterns

3. Data Storage

CalmSphere operates as a client-side application:

  • OAuth tokens are stored locally in your browser (session storage)
  • Video files are processed locally and not uploaded to our servers
  • We do not store your video content on our servers
  • Metadata is stored locally in your browser session

4. Third-Party Services

CalmSphere integrates with the following third-party services:

  • YouTube Data API: For video search and upload
  • Instagram Graph API: For video upload to Instagram Reels
  • TikTok Content Posting API: For video upload to TikTok
  • Pexels API: For stock video search
  • Pixabay API: For stock video search

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • All API communications use HTTPS
  • No sensitive data is transmitted to our servers

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data (OAuth tokens can be revoked through platform settings)
  • Delete your data (clear browser session storage)
  • Revoke API access at any time through platform settings

7. Cookies and Local Storage

CalmSphere uses browser local storage and session storage to:

  • Store OAuth tokens for authentication
  • Remember your preferences
  • Maintain session state

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our application.
